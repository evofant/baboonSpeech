---ExcelFile---
Dataset file contains results of exploratory modeling, performed in the APEX software (Stark et al., 1998). 

In articulatory modeling, three parameters were varied
-Jaw height varied between 1 (minimum) and 25 (maximum) in increments of 1. 
-Lips were varied as rounded or spread. 
-Larynx height was varied between 85 (minimum) and 110 (maximum in increcemens of 5.
